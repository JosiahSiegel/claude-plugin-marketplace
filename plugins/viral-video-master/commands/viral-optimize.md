# Platform-Specific Viral Optimization

## Purpose
Optimize a video for maximum viral potential on a specific platform with tailored, actionable recommendations.

## Workflow

### 1. Identify Target Platform

**Ask about:**
- Primary platform target
- Secondary platforms for repurposing
- Content type and topic
- Current stage (planning, editing, posted)
- Available resources and tools

## TikTok Optimization

### Algorithm Signals (2025)
| Signal | Weight | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Watch time | Highest | Optimize first 3 seconds |
| Completion rate | Very High | Keep under 60 seconds |
| Replays | High | Create loop-worthy content |
| Shares | High | Add share triggers |
| Comments | Medium | Include conversation starters |
| Likes | Lower (2025 update) | Still important, not primary |

### TikTok Checklist
- [ ] Video length: 15-30 seconds (peak) or under 60 seconds
- [ ] Aspect ratio: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- [ ] Hook in first 1.3 seconds
- [ ] Trending sound used
- [ ] Captions added and styled
- [ ] 3-5 relevant hashtags
- [ ] Micro-niche targeting
- [ ] Loop potential considered
- [ ] CTA included

### TikTok-Specific Tips
- **Cold Start**: First 200-500 viewers determine distribution
- **Sound**: 88% say sound is essential - never skip audio
- **Niche**: Algorithm favors micro-niche over broad appeal (2.5x better distribution)
- **Posting**: 1-4 times daily; 68% of views in first 24 hours
- **Challenges**: 41% participate monthly - join relevant ones

## YouTube Shorts Optimization

### Algorithm Signals
| Signal | Weight | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Watch time | Highest | Maximize retention |
| CTR | High | Thumbnail and title matter |
| Engagement | High | Likes, comments, shares |
| Subscriber conversion | Medium | CTA to subscribe |

### YouTube Shorts Checklist
- [ ] Video length: 50-60 seconds (sweet spot for 3x views)
- [ ] Aspect ratio: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- [ ] Hook in first 3 seconds
- [ ] Title optimized (max 100 chars, keyword-rich)
- [ ] Engaging thumbnail
- [ ] Captions/subtitles added

- [ ] Teases long-form content if applicable

### YouTube Shorts-Specific Tips
- **Length**: 55-second versions get 3x more views than 15-second
- **Long-form Combo**: Shorts + long-form = 41% faster channel growth
- **CTR**: Custom thumbnails = 60-70% higher CTR
- **Posting**: 1-3 times daily for growth

## YouTube Long-form Optimization

### Algorithm Signals
| Signal | Weight | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Watch time | Highest | Most critical metric |
| CTR | Very High | Thumbnail and title essential |
| Audience retention | Very High | Minimize drop-offs |
| Session time | High | Keep viewers on YouTube |
| Engagement | Medium | Comments, likes, shares |

### YouTube Long-form Checklist
- [ ] Video length: 8-15 minutes (sweet spot)
- [ ] Aspect ratio: 16:9 (1920x1080)
- [ ] Hook in first 5-10 seconds
- [ ] Custom thumbnail with face, emotion, <5 words
- [ ] Title: Primary keyword + compelling copy
- [ ] Description: 150-250 words, keyword in first 25
- [ ] Chapters/timestamps added
- [ ] End screen and cards added
- [ ] Closed captions (94% of top videos have them)
- [ ] Playlist placement considered

### YouTube Long-form Tips
- **Thumbnail**: Test up to 3 variants with Test & Compare feature
- **8+ minutes**: Enables more ad placements
- **Connected TV**: Long-form performing well on CTV
- **Retention**: 70%+ in first 30 seconds = much higher ranking
- **SEO**: Videos can get 2-5x more views from Google ranking

## Instagram Reels Optimization

### Algorithm Signals (December 2025)
| Signal | Weight | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Topic clarity | Very High | Algorithm needs to categorize |
| Engagement | High | Saves, shares, comments |
| Watch time | High | Completion rate matters |
| Follows from Reel | High | Content discovery |

### Instagram Reels Checklist
- [ ] Video length: 7-30 seconds (viral) or 30-90 seconds (engagement)
- [ ] Aspect ratio: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- [ ] 3/8/12 rule: Hook (3s), deepen (8s), deliver (12s+)
- [ ] Captions for sound-off viewing (50% watch without sound)
- [ ] 3-5 highly relevant hashtags
- [ ] Keyword-optimized caption
- [ ] Clear topic/niche focus
- [ ] CTA included

### Instagram Reels Tips
- **Trial Reels**: Test content without risking engagement metrics
- **Sound**: 50% watch without sound - captions essential
- **Topic Clarity**: December 2025 update emphasizes clear categorization
- **Best Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 11 AM - 6 PM

## Cross-Platform Optimization

### Repurposing Workflow
1. **Create primary version** for main platform
2. **Adjust length** for each platform's sweet spot
3. **Modify captions** for platform norms
4. **Update hashtags** per platform strategy
5. **Adjust aspect ratio** if needed (rare in 2025)

### Platform Priority by Content Type
| Content Type | Primary | Secondary | Tertiary |
|--------------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Entertainment | TikTok | Instagram | YouTube Shorts |
| Educational | YouTube Long | YouTube Shorts | TikTok |
| Product/Commerce | TikTok | Instagram | Facebook |
| Professional | LinkedIn | YouTube | Instagram |
| News/Trending | TikTok | Instagram | Twitter |

## Output Format

Provide:
1. **Platform-Specific Checklist** with completion status
2. **Optimization Score** current vs optimized
3. **Technical Specs** exact dimensions, length, format
4. **Content Adjustments** hook, pacing, CTA modifications
5. **Metadata Optimization** title, description, hashtags, tags
6. **Visual Recommendations** thumbnail, captions, graphics
7. **Posting Strategy** best times, frequency, cross-posting plan
8. **A/B Testing Plan** what to test for improvement
