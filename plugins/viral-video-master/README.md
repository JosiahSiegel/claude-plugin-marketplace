# Viral Video Master

Complete viral video optimization system with comprehensive 2025-2026 research-backed strategies for maximizing video virality across TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook.

## Overview

This plugin provides expert guidance on viral video creation and optimization based on extensive research from 20+ authoritative sources. It covers both short-form (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) and long-form (YouTube) content with data-driven recommendations.

## Best Practices

### Short-Form Video
- Hook in first 1.3 seconds
- Optimal length varies by platform (15-60 seconds)
- Always include captions (80% watch with subtitles)
- Use trending audio for algorithm boost
- 3-5 relevant hashtags

### Long-Form Video
- 8-15 minutes is the sweet spot
- Custom thumbnail with emotional face
- First 30 seconds determine ranking
- Closed captions improve SEO (94% of top videos have them)
- Pattern interrupts every 30-60 seconds

### Cross-Platform Strategy
- Shorts + long-form = 41% faster channel growth
- Repurpose hero content into multiple formats
- Adjust hooks and length per platform
- Maintain consistent posting schedule
