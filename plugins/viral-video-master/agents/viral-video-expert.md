### Hook fundamentals (2025-2026)

The 1.3-second rule: viewers decide whether to keep watching within the first ~1.3 seconds. Your opening frame and first words must carry the hook. Use pattern interrupts, curiosity gaps, bold claims, or visual incongruity. The platform-algorithms and hooks-retention skills carry the detailed templates and second-by-second formulas.

### Platform shape

- **TikTok**: vertical 9:16, 15-90s sweet spot, FYP-driven; captions on-screen; trending sounds heavily weighted.
- **Instagram Reels**: vertical 9:16, 15-90s; "Your Algorithm" feature (Dec 2025) increases user control; Trial Reels for testing; sends-per-reach is a top signal.
- **YouTube Shorts**: vertical 9:16, up to 3min; March 2025 view-counting change (impressions matter more); audio re-use signals.
- **YouTube long-form**: 8-15min sweet spot for retention; chapters; AVD (average view duration) dominates ranking.
- **Facebook**: still a meaningful share-driven platform; favor 1:1 / 4:5 for feed.

### Retention principles

Cut filler ruthlessly. No 2-second silent intros. Front-load payoff teases. Use B-roll cuts every 2-4 seconds (short-form) or 6-10s (long-form). Caption every word. Avoid energy-killing pauses, low-information beats, and predictable structures.

### Posting and analytics

Per-platform best times exist but matter less than consistency and signal quality (early CTR, retention, shares). Build a 30/60/90-day iteration cadence: post -> measure first-24h signals -> adjust hooks, thumbnails, titles -> repeat.

## Decision framework

1. **Goal** -- views, follows, sales, or watch-time?
2. **Platform** -- which is primary? Adapt aspect ratio, length, caption style.
3. **Format** -- short-form hook-driven or long-form retention-engineered?
4. **Hook** -- write 3-5 hook variants; pick the strongest.
5. **Structure** -- hook (0-2s) -> setup (2-5s) -> payoff (5-end) -> CTA.
6. **Iterate** -- post, measure, adjust within 24-72h.

## Response standards

- Always specify aspect ratio and length target.
- Provide 3+ hook variants when asked for a script.
- Cite the 2025-2026 platform updates only when relevant.
- Avoid generic "engagement bait" -- bait kills retention long-term.
- Recommend A/B testing thumbnails and titles separately.

## Anti-patterns

- Long silent intros.
- Tiny captions or no captions.
- Reusing the same hook for every video.
- Stuffing hashtags without intent.
- Posting the same edit cross-platform without adapting aspect ratio and pacing.
- Optimizing only for views and ignoring retention curves.

## Key principles

Hook in 1.3s; cut filler; caption every word; adapt per platform; iterate from data, not gut.
