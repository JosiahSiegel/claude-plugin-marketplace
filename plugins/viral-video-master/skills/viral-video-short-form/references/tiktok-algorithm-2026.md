# TikTok Algorithm Deep Dive (2026)

## Algorithm Mechanics

### Cold Start Testing

Every video goes through TikTok's cold start system:

1. **Initial Distribution**: 200-500 viewers
2. **Performance Measurement**: Compared against benchmarks
3. **Expansion Decision**: Strong metrics = broader reach
4. **Iterative Scaling**: Each tier unlocks larger audience

### Algorithm Signals (Ranked by Weight)

| Signal | Weight | What Algorithm Measures |
|--------|--------|------------------------|
| Watch Time | Highest | Total seconds watched per viewer |
| Completion Rate | Very High | % of video watched to end |
| Replays | High | Video watched multiple times |
| Shares | High | Direct shares via DM/external |
| Comments | Medium-High | Written engagement depth |
| Follows | Medium | New followers from video |
| Likes | Lower (2025 update) | Heart button taps |

**2025 Update**: Shallow interactions (likes) have reduced weight compared to deeper signals like watch time, replays, and shares.

### Micro-Niche Prioritization (2025-2026)

TikTok now heavily favors micro-niche content over broad appeal:

**How It Works**:
- AI micro-categorizes content: "beginner leg workouts for women" vs just "fitness"
- Specialized content sees **2.5x better distribution**
- Niche authority = more algorithm push for new content

**Strategy**:
1. Define specific niche clearly
2. Use niche-specific language and hashtags
3. Build authority in one area before expanding
4. Let algorithm understand your content category

### For You Page (FYP) Mechanics

| Stage | Audience Size | Success Criteria |

| Test 1 | 200-500 | First 3 seconds retention |
| Test 2 | 1,000-5,000 | Completion rate vs length |
| Test 3 | 10,000-50,000 | Early engagement velocity |

| Viral | 1M+ | All metrics strong |

## Content Preferences

### Algorithm Favors
- Family-friendly content
- Positive emotional tone
- Educational value
- Authentic creator content
- Micro-niche targeting
- Trending sound usage

### Algorithm Deprioritizes
- Controversial/negative content
- Duplicated content
- Low-quality production
- Misleading thumbnails
- Watermarked content from other platforms
- Content that triggers safety systems

## Hashtag Strategy

| Hashtag Type | Purpose | Examples |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| Broad/Trending | Reach attempt | #fyp, #viral, #foryou |
| Category | Topic discovery | #fitness, #cooking, #tech |
| Niche | Targeted audience | #beginneryoga, #airfryer |
| Branded | Campaign tracking | #YourBrandChallenge |

**Optimal Strategy**:
- 3-5 hashtags per video
- Mix of niche and category
- Quality over quantity
- Trending hashtags increase engagement 23%
