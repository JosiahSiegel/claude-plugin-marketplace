### Best Practices for TikTok Shop

**Product Video Structure**:
1. Hook with product in first 3 seconds
2. Show product in use
3. Highlight key benefit
4. Include price/offer
5. Clear CTA (Shop link)

**Optimal Length**: 15-30 seconds for product demos

**Content Types That Convert**:
- Unboxing videos
- Before/after demonstrations
- Tutorial with product
- "Day in my life" with product
- Comparison videos
- Review content

## Live Shopping

### Why Live Shopping Converts

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Real-time interaction | Trust building |
| Urgency | Limited-time offers |
| Demonstration | See product in action |
| Q&A | Address objections live |
| Entertainment | Engaging experience |

### Live Shopping Statistics

- Conversion rate: Up to **10x higher** than traditional e-commerce
- Average watch time: 10-20 minutes
- Repeat viewer rate: 40%+ for successful hosts

### Live Shopping Best Practices

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Duration | 30-60 minutes |
| Products featured | 5-10 per session |
| Engagement | Respond to comments constantly |
| Offers | Exclusive, time-limited |
| CTA | Clear, repeated throughout |
