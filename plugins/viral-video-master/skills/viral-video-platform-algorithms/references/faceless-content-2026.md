## Content Formats

### Stock Footage + Voiceover

**Structure**:
1. Write script
2. Generate/record voiceover
3. Select stock footage
4. Add captions and effects
5. Publish

**Tools**: Pexels, Storyblocks, Shutterstock

### Screen Recording

**Best For**:
- Tutorial content
- Software reviews
- Gaming content
- Reaction videos

**Tools**: OBS, Loom, Screenflow

### Animation

**Best For**:
- Explainer videos
- Educational content
- Story-driven content

**Tools**: Vyond, Animaker, Powtoon

### AI-Generated Visuals

## Benefits of Faceless Content

### Practical Benefits

- **Privacy protection**: No personal exposure
- **Scalability**: Easier to produce at volume
- **Creative freedom**: Not limited to personal brand
- **Multiple channels**: Can run several simultaneously
- **Outsourceable**: Easy to hire help
- **Time flexibility**: No filming required
- **Location independent**: Work from anywhere

### Strategic Benefits

- Lower barrier to entry
- Faster content production
- Easier A/B testing
- Less equipment needed
- No on-camera anxiety
- Simpler editing workflow

## Common Mistakes

### What to Avoid

| Mistake | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Low-quality AI voice | Invest in premium TTS or human voice |
| Generic stock footage | Select unique, relevant clips |
| No personality | Develop consistent style and tone |
| Poor pacing | Edit tightly, maintain energy |
| Ignoring SEO | Optimize titles, descriptions, tags |
| Inconsistent upload | Establish and maintain schedule |
